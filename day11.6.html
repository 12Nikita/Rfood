About Us


In regions of South Asia, desi in the context of food, implies “native” or “traditional”. Common examples are “desi ghee”, which is the traditional clarified butter used in South Asia as opposed to more processed fats such as vegetable oils. “Desi chicken” may mean a native breed of chicken.Our restaraunt here is carrying on the leggacy of traditional indian food recipes includen the finger licking good recipes of the mughal times, we want the people of our country to get the taste of these lost recipes so that they could remember the taste of their own country that has been and is famous for its uniqueness in spices along with its diverse culture and religion.Its the beginning of this new era where people will get to know about all the desi indian tastes they are not yet aware of and also the recipes that have been lost all this time,the recipes here our are made with love and care, hygeine and beauty of indian culture since the main chefs of the restraunt are the mothers,who have the best experince of in life we all know that the even the best chef of the world loves their mothers handmade food ,so with this concept of spreading this mothers love and care in the form of food.You can have the plain normal food you used to eat in your home with the same taste upto the spicy delicious recipes made with proper hygeine.

What is special about Indian food? Indian cooks blend a variety of spices to create the unique flavor of their dishes. Their foods are highly aromatic and very tasty because of the combination of different spices, such as turmeric, pepper, and more.

What are the advantages of Desi food? Indian Food Provides all Important Nutrients It fuels your body and helps your body produce energy. Pulses are rich in Vitamins A, B, C and E. They also contain a good proportion of minerals like potassium, iron, and zinc. Rice and flour fulfils the required carbohydrate, protein, and fiber consumption of the body.
